Darling Harbour

The Darling Harbour presents several recreational and entertainment events and attractions (it is an attraction made up of attractions!)With Australian animals at the SEA LIFE Sydney Aquarium and the WILD LIFE Sydney Zoo, waterfront dining in the several restaurants that it offers, entertaining attractions, like the Ferris Wheel and the nighttime fireworks, intriguing history and exciting nightlife, your day will never feel boring here! As one of Sydney’s largest dining, shopping and entertainment precincts, it has a full calendar of events.
