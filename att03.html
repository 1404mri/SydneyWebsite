Taronga Zoo

The Torango Zoo has over 4,000 animals, with over 350 species including Koalas, Tree Kangaroos, Sumatran Tigers, and more. It first opened in October 7,1916, and has had an increasing number of visitors since then, being the most popular experience in Sydney as of 2010.
