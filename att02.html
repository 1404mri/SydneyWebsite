Royal Botanic Garden

The Royal Botanic Garden in Sydney is a state-funded botanic garden. It is right along the Sydney Harbor, near the Sydney Opera House, with it occupying more than 66 acres. It has about 5,000 different kinds of plants, while the native species of Australia is emphasized the most.
